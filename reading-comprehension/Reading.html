Reading comprehension is the ability to process text and understand its meaning. At On Screen School, we provide vetted resources to help you integrate knowledge with what you already know.

Example Passage

Every morning, Aria walked past the old clock tower on her way to school. The hands had stopped at 11:47 decades ago, yet the townspeople left it untouched. To Aria, the frozen clock felt like a secret kept by the city.

Two Kinds of Questions

Explicit: What time do the clock hands show? (11:47)

Implicit: Why is the clock untouched? (Historical value)
