# Math & Vocabulary Learning Modules

A self-contained, interactive learning suite for students to master **Math (Decimals & Mixed Numbers)** and **Vocabulary (in Context)**. No installation, no internet, no accounts — just open and learn.

---

## 🚀 How to Set Up

### Math Module
1. **Download the `math` folder** as a `.zip` file.
2. **Extract** the `.zip` file to your computer.
3. **Open `math/intro.html`** by double-clicking it. It will open in your web browser.
4. Read the introduction, then click **\"Start the Quiz\"** to begin practicing decimals and mixed numbers.

### Vocabulary Module
1. **Download the `vocabulary` folder** as a `.zip` file.
2. **Extract** the `.zip` file to your computer.
3. **Open `vocabulary/intro.html`** by double-clicking it. It will open in your web browser.
4. Read the introduction, then click **\"Start the Quiz\"** to begin vocabulary practice.

> **Tip for Teachers & Parents:** Both modules work entirely offline. Save the folders for easy classroom or home access.

---

## 🎓 5 Tips for Students

1. **Practice daily.** Spend 10-15 minutes on Math or Vocabulary — consistency beats cramming.
2. **Read carefully.** In both modules, understanding the intro examples helps you ace the quiz.
3. **Use feedback.** Wrong answer? Read the explanation and retry to build skills.
4. **Apply what you learn.** Try decimals in shopping or new words in sentences outside the quiz.
5. **Stay relaxed.** Take breaks if frustrated — fresh eyes solve problems faster.

---

## 🍎 5 Tips for Teachers & Homeschool Parents

1. **Start with intros.** Direct students to `intro.html` in each folder first for key concepts.
2. **Offline ready.** Perfect for low-tech environments; share folders via USB/email.
3. **Self-paced learning.** Instant feedback per question means minimal supervision needed.
4. **Combine modules.** Alternate Math and Vocabulary sessions for balanced review.
5. **Review results.** Discuss quiz feedback together to reinforce learning.
